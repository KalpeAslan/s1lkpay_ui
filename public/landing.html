How it works

From payment link
to fiat — in seconds

No bank wires. No crypto complexity for your customers. Create a link, let them pay in any supported asset, and receive your preferred currency — automatically converted and settled.

01 / 04

Create a payment link

Set the amount, choose the currency you want to receive (fiat or crypto), add a description and deadline. Your link or QR code is ready instantly.

02 / 04

Customer pays in crypto

Your customer pays using any supported asset — USDC, USDT, SOL, KZTE/EVO, or PEPE. They scan the QR or click the link and pay from their wallet in one tap.

03 / 04

Auto-conversion if needed

If the customer pays with a different asset than your preferred settlement currency, our system handles the conversion automatically — no manual swaps, no rate risk.

04 / 04

Receive your preferred currency

Funds land in your wallet as your chosen currency — stablecoin, SOL, or fiat. Withdraw anytime or let balances accumulate across payment links.

Features

Payment infrastructure for the borderless economy
